Then and now

Artistic power struggles.

Early days

A German craftsman named Johannes Gutenberg developed a way to automate book copying via a press. This process utilizes an inked top and a lower platen below which in turn. This helped produce books quickly and accurately that could produce 250 pages.

First editorial

Dates back in 1754, a cartoon by Benjamin Franklin is one of the first well known illustration in a newspaper cartoon. Entitled as "Join or die", it depicts a snake divided in eight pieces which represents the eight colonies. This often appears in every conflict through the revolutionary war at that time.

James Gillray

Dubbed as the father of the political cartoon, One of the most influential political cartoonists of all time and is responsible for satirizing Napoleon and the reign of George III.

Thomas Nast.

Ever since the invention of the pen, these art styles were characterized with hatching and cross hatching. One of the famous examples is Thomas Nast, with his style of persuasive cartoons, he managed to affect the outcome of six different elections based off his activism. He is attributed to invent a stereotypical businessman in character in suit that is obese and is driven by greed.

These illustrations have known to be a dangerous game

Throughout history, these illustrators risk reprisal as they often poke fun or critique the status quo. This is what happened with the 19th century french lithographer Honore Daumer which he portrays the king as a grotesque giant that is exploiting the hard work of his people. The king fined and imprisoned the artist then banned his cartoons.

Highlights the feels of history

The Philippine free press (PFP), which dates back during the martial law, were few of the publications that depict the events during the time period about the atrocities of the administration even before the time of the internet and made great mediums in expressing owns views and opinions. The style seemed to be influenced by 70s or 80s era filipino made comics.

modern day

The rise of political turmoil during the COVID 19 pandemic and the succeeding administration help gave rise to the popularity of the art style online. This made artists adapt to the modern times and use a digital art style, even going so far to adapt memes and other popular culture into their work. This help develop solidarity among fellow artists and their followers at online spaces.
